Questions

Things people ask.

What photos do I need to provide?
3–5 clear photos of yourself in good lighting. Front-facing and 3/4 angle work best. You don't need professional photos to start — everyday phone shots are fine.
Can I specify any location or scene?
Yes. You can choose from our growing scene library or describe a custom environment. We'll confirm feasibility before you pay.
How does payment work?
We use PayPal. You'll receive a unique reference code — include it in your payment note so we can verify and begin your order immediately.
What's the actual resolution of outputs?
Starter delivers 1080px (ideal for Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn). Creator Pack delivers 2K. Professional delivers true 4K, suitable for print and large-format display.
Is this ethical to use for social media?
Absolutely — with honest use. We produce images of you, in places you could plausibly be, for creative brand expression. The same as any studio photography. We do not support deceptive use.
Do I own the images?
Personal and commercial use is included in Creator Pack and Professional tiers. Starter images are for personal/non-commercial use only.
